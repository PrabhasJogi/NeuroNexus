About NeuroNexus

Welcome to NeuroNexus Innovations, a pioneer in the field of IT services and software development. We are a team of dedicated professionals committed to delivering cutting-edge technological solutions.

Our expertise lies in developing and producing neural interface products such as microelectrode arrays, electrophysiology analysis, spike sorting, recording, and stimulation to support electrophysiology experiments. We started our journey in the commercial silicon probe field in 2004 and have been leading ever since.
